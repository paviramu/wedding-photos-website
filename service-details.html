Service Details

Personalised Pre & Post Wedding Shoot

Everyone is unique, just as their take on love

There would be one special trait unique to your relationship. We'll work that out and create your love story. We don't have any ready-made scripts for you because we do not believe in creating something to which you cannot relate. Because it is your special day, and you are about to join on a wonderful adventure with your companion.

Then how do we work?

We work on scripting and finalising the theme for your pre-wedding and post-wedding shoots while keeping the essence of your relationship in mind. It's like making the best wine for you that tastes the best over time. We have a team dedicated to personalised pre and post wedding shoots that will guide and assist you throughout the process to ensure that the end result will astonish you.

Pictures full of life

What makes our candids special?

Candids are special because of their uniqueness and they can directly take you back to the special day once you see them. We know you deserve the best, so we provide you with the best. Our team has the top photographers in Kerala who can sense the right moment to click the best moments.

Conceptual Films

A unique theme for the UNIQUE YOU

We value each person for who they are. Every person you meet is unique. Then why not express it visually? This thought gave rise to the notion of conceptual films. We apply this to our different services like bridal shower, pre and post wedding shoot, family tale series, and so on. To everyone's surprise, the idea was well received and has since become our best service.

How do we execute?

We develop a theme based on the mood of the upcoming event, the client's perspective, and the relationship trait they wish to project. The theme will be custom-written for the client. Our team will assist the clients with the costumes and location for the shoot once the theme has been finalised. Our team is well equipped with ideas and technology. We have musicians, dubbing artists, and art directors to make the visuals the best. Everything we can guarantee is your satisfaction. Want to create your own conceptual film? Don't wait anymore, just dial us !
